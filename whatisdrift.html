What is drift and its varieties?

Drift is a type of extreme high-speed motorsport, which has a lot of fans.
It is characterized by a deliberate breakdown of the rear axle during a turn,
its passage in a controlled skid at the highest possible speed.
The angle of the rear wheels during drifting exceeds the angle of the front wheels,
sometimes significantly.
This type of driving speaks to the highest skill of the driver,
his ability to control the power of the car and direct it in the right direction.

Varieties

There are many techniques, and sometimes only a professional can distinguish one from the other.
Hand-breaking drifting
One of the simplest, despite the frightening name.
Suitable for beginners, the first steps in drifting.
The main advantage is that if you make a mistake,
you can correct it with corrective jerks.
Execution: the clutch is squeezed out,
a powerful jerk of the handbrake is made, the car skids.
Then the clutch pedal is released.
Hand-breaking drifting trains an extreme driver to control the strength and speed of the jerk.
Clutch-Kick drifting
The clutch is squeezed out and rushes sharply.
It is because of this that the car skids just as sharply.
Yorin drifting
During this variation, all wheels are disrupted at once,
which occurs in the middle of a bend in the track.
Kanteria drifting
The rotation is carried out in the shape of the letter "S".
In Russian, you can call it a swing, a whip.
Kanteria drifting looks incredibly impressive.
Braking drifting
